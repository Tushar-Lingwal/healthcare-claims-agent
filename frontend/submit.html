Tips for accurate results
📝Write clinical notes as you would in an EMR — the NER extracts diagnoses automatically without tags.
🏷️Use diagnosis/procedure tags to supplement free text — especially useful for abbreviated terms the NER might miss.
💳BASIC plan rejects elective joint replacement and advanced MRI. Use STANDARD or PREMIUM for those procedures.
🔐Patient names and dates are encrypted before any AI processing — no PHI leaves the vault.
